How to Play Keno

Keno is a lottery-style game where you select numbers and hope they match the drawn numbers.

  • Select 1-15 numbers from 1-80
  • Place your bet amount
  • 20 numbers are drawn every 3 minutes
  • Win based on how many of your numbers match the drawn numbers
  • Payouts increase dramatically with more matches

Payout Example: Pick 10 numbers, match all 10 = 25,000x your bet!
